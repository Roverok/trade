Trade

Trade is a mobile web app where you can buy and sell stocks stocks at what should be approximately their current price, using virtual currency. The idea is to either pratice, try out theories, or just have fun.

To install it, visit http://trade.kevinbedi.com on a mobile device.

For the best experience, add it to your homescreen if your device allows it - this should be simple on Android/iPhone.
